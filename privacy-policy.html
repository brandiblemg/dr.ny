Overview

This policy describes how information may be collected and used when you visit this website or contact us. It is intended to complement our Professional Services Disclaimer and Terms of Service.

Not for medical treatment

Information you submit through this website (including contact forms, scheduling tools, or email) is used for inquiries, scheduling, and professional services administration. It is not collected or used to provide medical diagnosis, treatment, prescribing, or emergency care. Do not submit urgent medical information through this site.

Information we may collect

We may collect information you voluntarily provide (such as name, email, role, and message content), basic technical data (such as browser type and pages visited), and scheduling details if you use an integrated booking tool.

How we use information

We use information to respond to inquiries, coordinate conversations, improve the website, and administer professional engagements. We do not sell personal information.

Sharing

We may share information with service providers who help operate the website or scheduling tools, when required by law, or with your consent. Those providers are expected to protect information consistent with this policy.

Retention and security

We retain information only as long as needed for the purposes described above or as required by law. We use reasonable safeguards, but no method of transmission over the internet is completely secure.

Your choices

You may contact us to request access, correction, or deletion of personal information where applicable law allows. You may also opt out of non-essential communications.
