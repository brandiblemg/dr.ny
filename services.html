How We Work

Services

Three pathways—Patient Engagement, Pharmaceutical Consulting, and Healthcare Provider Training—grounded in the ACE Framework.

Patient Engagement Practice

Biopsychosocial model (pyramid structure)

We listen to the whole person—not just symptoms. Base: coping mechanisms. Middle: modifiable factors (sleep, diet, stress). Top: medication. Process includes pre-visit consultation to identify fears, mistrust, and knowledge gaps; preparation of 3 key questions for your physician; optional remote accompaniment during visits; and post-visit education and reinforcement.

Dr. Youssef is not replacing physicians—he ensures patients are heard, informed, and empowered.

  • ✓ ~20 focus group sessions over 18 months with successful engagement outcomes
  • ✓ Ongoing engagement, not one-time consultation

Pharmaceutical Consulting

Study design with patient-centered outcomes

We understand unmet needs through patient groups and key opinion leaders to shape meaningful study design. Example: Pediatric Brain Tumor Society collaboration—advocating for daily function measurements (e.g., child’s ability to self-feed), compassionate study protocols, and educating pharma partners on real patient priorities.

  • ✓ Patient advisory boards and KOL engagement
  • ✓ Authentic recruitment narratives

Healthcare Provider Training

Empathetic engagement and patient-centered communication

Target audience: nurses, physician assistants, medical students, residents, and young physicians. We address diminished communication skills due to reliance on AI and algorithms by teaching empathetic engagement and patient-centered communication—e.g., training providers to explain the “why” behind recommendations (beyond labels like “heart health”).

  • ✓ Interactive workshops grounded in neuroscience
  • ✓ Measurable shifts in satisfaction and trust
