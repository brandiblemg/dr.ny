Service Path

Pharmaceutical & Biotech Consultation

Patient-centered consulting for study design, engagement strategy, and meaningful outcomes.

Overview

Built from the ACE framework, this path helps pharma and biopharma teams translate patient voice into practical strategy. We bring real-world perspective into protocol design, recruitment language, and educational narratives so research is both rigorous and human-centered.

What this path includes

  • • Study design support with patient-centered outcomes and burden awareness
  • • Advisory engagement with patient groups and key opinion leaders
  • • Message and recruitment strategy grounded in lived patient priorities

Who this serves

Pharma and biopharma leaders, medical affairs teams, clinical development groups, and cross-functional partners building patient-first programs.

Pharmaceutical Consulting

Study design with patient-centered outcomes

We understand unmet needs through patient groups and key opinion leaders to shape meaningful study design. Example: Pediatric Brain Tumor Society collaboration—advocating for daily function measurements (e.g., child’s ability to self-feed), compassionate study protocols, and educating pharma partners on real patient priorities.
