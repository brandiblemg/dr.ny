Service Path

Mentoring & Leadership Coaching

Training healthcare professionals in empathetic, patient-centered communication.

Overview

This path helps clinicians and care teams reconnect communication quality with outcomes. Through practical workshops and scripts, teams learn how to explain the why behind care plans, build trust faster, and make encounters more human without slowing care delivery.

What this path includes

  • • Workshops on empathetic communication and patient-centered dialogue
  • • Practical scripts for explaining rationale, risks, and next steps
  • • Team-level habits that improve trust, clarity, and follow-through

Who this serves

Nurses, physician assistants, medical students, residents, and early-career physicians seeking stronger communication outcomes.

Healthcare Provider Training

Empathetic engagement and patient-centered communication

Target audience: nurses, physician assistants, medical students, residents, and young physicians. We address diminished communication skills due to reliance on AI and algorithms by teaching empathetic engagement and patient-centered communication—e.g., training providers to explain the “why” behind recommendations (beyond labels like “heart health”).

  • ✓ Interactive workshops grounded in neuroscience
  • ✓ Measurable shifts in satisfaction and trust
